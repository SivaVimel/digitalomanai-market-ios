What it does

Productivity, conversation, and content tools — all in one privacy-respecting app.

Conversational AI

Natural multi-turn chat with rich responses. Works with both local and cloud models — same interface, same speed.

Runs on-device

A built-in local model means you can chat with AI even on a plane, in a tunnel, or wherever the network isn't.

Web search

Live results filtered by news, location, images, or video. The AI reads results in real time and cites sources.

PDF & file understanding

Drop a PDF, image, or text file into the chat. The app extracts content and feeds it to your AI for summaries, Q&A, or analysis.

Vision & OCR

Send a photo for instant analysis. Vision-capable models read it directly; otherwise on-device OCR extracts text and hands it off.

Voice transcription

Record speech for real-time transcription. AI summarizes the result and optionally saves it back to your Notes.

Mail in-app

Bring Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, iCloud — or any IMAP/SMTP mailbox. Read, summarize, compose, and smart-reply directly inside the app. App passwords stay on-device — we never see them.

Notes & to-do

AI-enhanced notes with side-by-side polished versions. Date-aware tasks that quietly inform every prompt for context.

03

Privacy isn't a feature.
It's the foundation.

Your data never touches our servers.

Conversations, API keys, mail account credentials, notes, and to-do items live exclusively on your device. We don't collect, mirror, or back them up anywhere. No ads, no analytics, no tracking SDKs — we don't even know you're using it.

When you chat with the bundled local model, nothing leaves your phone — not the message, not the model output. When you use a cloud provider, the request goes directly from your device to their API using your key. We're not in the middle.

The local model itself is downloaded from a public CDN on first launch (so the install size stays small) and then runs entirely offline.
